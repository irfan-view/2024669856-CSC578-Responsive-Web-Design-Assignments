Dr. Mahathir Mohamad

The Architect of Modern Malaysia

A Timeline of Tun Dr. Mahathir's Life:

  • 1925 - Born in Alor Setar, Kedah.
  • 1947 - Entered King Edward VII College of Medicine in Singapore to study medicine.
  • 1957 - Opened his own private medical practice, Maha Clinic, in his hometown.
  • 1964 - Elected as a Member of Parliament for the first time representing Kota Star Selatan.
  • 1976 - Appointed as the Deputy Prime Minister of Malaysia.
  • 1981 - Sworn in as the 4th Prime Minister of Malaysia, beginning a transformative 22-year tenure.
  • 1991 - Introduced "Vision 2020" (Wawasan 2020), aiming to make Malaysia a fully developed nation.
  • 1998 - Famously pegged the Malaysian ringgit to the US dollar to protect the economy during the Asian Financial Crisis.
  • 2003 - Stepped down from office, passing the leadership to Abdullah Ahmad Badawi.
  • 2018 - Returned to politics and was sworn in as the 7th Prime Minister at the age of 92, making him the world's oldest serving leader at the time.

"To change the world, you must first change yourself, then your community, and finally the nation."

-- Dr. Mahathir Mohamad
